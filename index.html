Methodology Overview

Netzerra implements IPCC 2006 Guidelines updated with AR6 (2021) GWP₁₀₀ values. Calculations follow the GHG Protocol Scope 1/2/3 framework aligned with ISO 14064-1:2018.

Emissions (tCO₂e) = Σ [Activity Data × Emission Factor × GWP₁₀₀] ÷ 1000
GWP₁₀₀(CH₄) = 27.9, GWP₁₀₀(N₂O) = 273 — IPCC AR6 WG1 Ch.7

Borehole Methodology

Scope 1: diesel (drilling + pumping + generator). Scope 2: KPLC grid × (1 − solar%). Scope 3: casing materials amortised over design lifetime.

S1 = (L_drill/lifetime + L_pump + L_gen) × 2.68
S2 = kWh × 0.497 × (1 − solar%)
S3 = (kg_steel×1850 + kg_PVC×2410 + kg_cement×830) / lifetime / 1000
Total [tCO₂e/yr] = (S1 + S2 + S3) / 1000

Livestock Methodology

IPCC 2006 Vol.4 Ch.10 Tier 1 — Eastern Africa defaults. Enteric CH₄ + manure CH₄ + pasture N₂O (EF3 = 0.02). AR6 GWP₁₀₀ values applied.

E_enteric = Σ(heads × EF_ent) × 27.9
E_manure_CH4 = Σ(heads × EF_man_CH4) × 27.9
E_manure_N2O = Σ(heads × Nex × 0.02 × 44/28) × 273
Total [tCO₂e/yr] = (E_ent + E_man + E_n2o + E_feed + E_elec) / 1000

Transport Methodology

DEFRA/BEIS 2023 fuel-based method. Refrigerant fugitives use IPCC AR6 GWP values.

E_fuel = Σ(L_fuel × EF_fuel) / 1000
E_ref = (kg_HFC134a × 1430 + kg_R404A × 3922) / 1000
Total [tCO₂e/yr] = E_fuel + E_ref

Construction Methodology

Scope 3 embodied carbon (Bath ICE v3.0 cradle-to-gate) dominates most projects. Site diesel = Scope 1. Grid electricity = Scope 2.

S3 = t_cement×830 + m³_conc×159 + t_steel×1850 + t_rebar×1990 + ...
S1 = (L_excav + L_gen) × months × 2.68 + truck_km × 0.20
S2 = kWh/mo × months × 0.497

Manufacturing Methodology

IPCC 2006 Vol.3. Intensity benchmarked against KTDA, IFC, FAO, World Steel sector references. CBAM-ready output.

S1 = L_diesel×2.68 + L_HFO×3.17 + kg_LPG×2.98 + t_CO2×1000
S2 = (kWh_grid − kWh_solar) × 0.497
S3 = kg_HFC×1430 + kg_R404A×3922 + m³_ww×25 + t_waste×580
Intensity = Total_kgCO₂e ÷ tonnes_output

Netzerra Score Formula

The NTZ Score (0–100) rewards intensity reduction, active offsetting, year-on-year improvement, and engagement. Aligned with SBTi 1.5°C pathway (7% annual reduction).

Intensity_Score = 40 × max(0, 1 − E_intensity / sector_benchmark)
Offset_Score = 30 × min(1, total_offsets / total_emissions)
Reduction_Score = 20 × max(0, ΔE_reduction / 0.07)
Engagement_Score = 10 × min(1, projects_tracked / 10)
NTZ_Score = Σ components [0–100]

The Story Behind Netzerra

Netzerra started as a single question during my industrial attachment at the Kenya Water Industry Association (KWIA) in 2023: "How much carbon does drilling a borehole actually emit?"

I watched drilling rigs burn diesel for days. Steel casings shipped hundreds of kilometres. Cement grout packed into every hole. No one was measuring any of it. I built a small spreadsheet — then a bigger one.

The late Mr. Tom Armstrong, then Chairman of KWIA, became my first mentor. He saw what I was doing and pushed me to think bigger — beyond boreholes, to every sector of Kenya's economy. His passing left a profound gap, and a profound responsibility to see this through.

Three years later, that spreadsheet is Netzerra — covering five sectors, 47 counties, KNCR-native architecture, and the only platform in Kenya that automates Community Development Agreements under the Carbon Markets Regulations 2024. Built by one person, in Kenya, for Kenya.
